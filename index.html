The Game

What is FlowSlayer?

Inspired by Hades, Devil May Cry, Dead Cells, and Furi — FlowSlayer fuses deep combat mechanics with tight roguelite structure.

⚔️

Deep Combat

20+ attacks, chorded inputs, combo windows, cancel mechanics, guard system — designed for players who want to master every tool available.

🎲

Roguelite Loop

20–30 min runs across 4 escalating arenas. Upgrade your modular weapon, level up every 5 milestones, discover synergies — start over if you fall.

⚡

C++ Precision

~95% native C++ on Unreal Engine 5.7.4. Blueprints only for asset wiring. Every frame of response engineered from scratch.

Core Mechanic

The Flow System

Flow is the heartbeat of FlowSlayer. Aggressive play charges your meter — every hit landed pushes you closer to an unstoppable state. But every dash costs Flow, and every hit you take drains it.

At maximum Flow, you become temporarily immune to all damage. Master the balance between relentless offense and resource management.

Tips

Dash can cancel any attack during its cancel window — use it to extend combos
Aerial attacks have gravity suppressed mid-execution (Air Stall)
Guard automatically breaks on dash or jump — no manual release needed
At max Flow you're immune — this is when you should go all-in
